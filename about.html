I grew up exploring the bluffs and backwaters of the Mississippi River in Minnesota's Driftless Area and have always had a love for the natural world and the maps that guide us through it.

I hold a degree in Geographical Information Systems from the University of St. Thomas (24'), where I was a member of the Earth, Environment, and Society Department, and I am a licensed Part 107 UAV pilot under the FAA.

After taking a year and a half break during which I lived and worked in the Columbia River Gorge, Alphabet City of Manhattan, and the Big Island of Hawai ªi, I am eager to return to geospatial analysis and cartography.
